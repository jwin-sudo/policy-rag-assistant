# Acceptable Use Policy

Document Owner: IT and Legal
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Review Cycle: Annual
Applies To: Use of company networks, devices, systems, and data

## 1. Purpose
This policy describes acceptable and prohibited use of company technology resources.

## 2. Acceptable Use Principles
Company systems are provided to support business objectives. Limited personal use is permitted if it is lawful, secure, and does not interfere with job responsibilities.

## 3. Prohibited Activities
Users must not:
- Circumvent security controls
- Share credentials or MFA tokens
- Download unapproved software on managed devices
- Access or distribute illegal or offensive material
- Use company systems for unauthorized commercial activity
- Exfiltrate or misuse confidential information

## 4. Email and Messaging
Use professional communication standards. Suspected phishing emails must be reported immediately. Auto-forwarding to personal accounts is prohibited.

## 5. Software and Licensing
Only licensed and approved software may be used. Users may not copy proprietary software or violate license terms.

## 6. Data Storage
Store work data in approved systems with appropriate access controls. External storage media use is restricted and may require encryption.

## 7. Internet and Network Use
Reasonable browsing is allowed. Accessing high-risk or malicious websites, using unauthorized proxies, or bypassing content controls is prohibited.

## 8. Monitoring Notice
The company may monitor usage logs, security events, and system activity for lawful security and compliance purposes.

## 9. Reporting Suspected Misuse
Users must report suspected policy violations, unauthorized access, and security concerns through approved channels.

## 10. Enforcement
Violations may result in access restrictions, disciplinary action, contract termination, or legal action depending on severity.

## 11. Acknowledgment
All users must acknowledge this policy during onboarding and after significant revisions.

## 12. Exceptions
Exceptions require written approval from IT and Legal with documented controls and expiration.
