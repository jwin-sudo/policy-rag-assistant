# Onboarding and Offboarding SOP

Document Owner: People Operations and IT
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Review Cycle: Annual
Applies To: Employees, interns, and contractors with system access

## 1. Purpose
This SOP provides standardized onboarding and offboarding steps to reduce operational risk and ensure consistent employee experience.

## 2. Onboarding Timeline
### T-7 to T-1 Days
- HR confirms role, manager, and start date.
- IT prepares device and baseline access.
- Hiring manager prepares 30-60-90 day plan.

### Day 1
- Employee completes policy acknowledgments.
- Manager introduces team norms and priorities.
- IT verifies login, MFA, and collaboration tool access.

### Week 1
- Role-specific access granted and validated.
- Required trainings assigned.
- Initial goals documented.

## 3. Required New Hire Training
- Code of Conduct and anti-harassment
- Security awareness and phishing
- Data privacy basics
- Role-specific compliance modules where applicable

## 4. Access Provisioning
Baseline access is automated via HRIS integration. Additional access requires ticketed request and owner approval.

## 5. Equipment Assignment
IT records asset serial numbers and assignment date. Employee confirms receipt and acceptable use obligations.

## 6. Probation and Checkpoints
Managers hold formal check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days. Progress and support needs are documented.

## 7. Offboarding Triggers
Offboarding starts upon resignation, termination decision, contract end, or role transfer requiring full access reset.

## 8. Offboarding Workflow
- HR confirms final working date and notifies stakeholders.
- IT schedules access revocation.
- Manager completes handover and knowledge transfer.
- Asset return is coordinated and tracked.

## 9. Access Revocation Standards
For involuntary terminations, access is revoked at notification time or earlier. For voluntary departures, access is revoked at end of last working day unless approved transition requires shorter timeline.

## 10. Asset Return and Data Transfer
Employees return all devices, badges, and hardware tokens. Business files are transferred to team-owned repositories before separation.

## 11. Exit Process
Exit interviews are offered to gather feedback on role clarity, management support, and workplace experience.

## 12. Documentation and Audit
All onboarding and offboarding actions are logged in HRIS and IT ticketing systems for auditability.

## 13. Exceptions
Any deviation requires written approval from People Operations and Security.
