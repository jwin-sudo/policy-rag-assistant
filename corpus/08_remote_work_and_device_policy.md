# Remote Work and Device Policy

Document Owner: IT and People Operations
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Review Cycle: Annual
Applies To: All remote and hybrid workers

## 1. Purpose
This policy sets security and operational requirements for remote work to protect data and maintain productivity.

## 2. Eligibility
Remote work eligibility is role-dependent and approved by management. Some roles require periodic on-site attendance for operational or compliance reasons.

## 3. Work Environment Requirements
Remote workers must maintain:
- A private workspace suitable for confidential calls
- Reliable internet connectivity
- Safe electrical setup and ergonomic workstation
- Ability to attend core collaboration hours

## 4. Company Device Requirements
Employees must use company-managed devices for company work unless exception is approved. Mandatory controls include:
- Full disk encryption
- Endpoint protection
- Auto-lock after inactivity
- Up-to-date operating system and browsers

## 5. Network and Connectivity
Use trusted networks whenever possible. Public Wi-Fi requires VPN with MFA. Open or unsecured networks without VPN are prohibited for company data access.

## 6. Data Handling for Remote Work
- Store files only in approved company systems.
- Do not print Confidential or Restricted data unless required and approved.
- Use company collaboration tools for file exchange.
- Avoid discussing sensitive matters in public spaces.

## 7. Physical Security
Devices must not be left unattended in public. When traveling, devices should remain in carry-on luggage and use privacy screens in shared environments.

## 8. Home Office Reimbursement
Eligible equipment and internet stipends follow People Operations guidelines. Expense claims require receipts and manager approval.

## 9. Incident Reporting
Lost devices, suspected phishing, and unauthorized access attempts must be reported within one hour. IT may remotely lock or wipe devices when risk is high.

## 10. Monitoring and Privacy
The company may monitor system access and security events on company-managed assets. Monitoring is limited to lawful business purposes and handled with proportionality.

## 11. Performance and Communication
Remote employees are expected to:
- Keep calendar and status current
- Participate in planned team rituals
- Provide timely updates on blocked tasks
- Meet role performance expectations

## 12. International Remote Work
Working from a different country may trigger tax, employment, or data transfer obligations and requires prior approval from HR and Legal.

## 13. Policy Violations
Violations may result in loss of remote work eligibility, device access restrictions, or disciplinary action.

## 14. Exceptions
Exceptions require written approval and must define controls, owner, and expiration date.
